Sleep

Why you should sleep 8 hours or more every night

  • You will have better processed memories
  • Sleep helps to keep your immune system robust
  • Good sleep can maximize your physical performance
  • Enhanced concentration and productivity
  • Prevent weight gain
  • Better sleep might mean better mood for some people
  • Improves memory and your brain might be sharper

How to fall asleep faster

  • 4-7-8: Breathe in 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, and exhale 8 seconds
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before sleeping
  • Sleeping at the same time every day
  • Breathing exercises and meditation
  • Military technique: relax your body head to toe and think of peace
  • Exercising or even yoga
