Journaling



What is Journaling

Journaling generally involves the practice of keeping a diary or journal that explores thoughts and feelings surrounding the events of your life.
There are several different ways to do this. Journaling, as a stress management and self-exploration tool, works best when done consistently,
but even occasional, sporadic journaling can be stress relieving when the practice is focused on gratitude or emotional processing.

Benefits

  • Journaling decreases the symptoms of asthma, arthritis, and other health conditions
  • It improves cognitive functioning
  • It can strengthen immune system response
  • It can counteract many of the negative effects of stress
  • Journaling can help boost your mood
  • Journaling can also strengthen other emotional functions


Tips on Journaling

  • Start small and write more as you progress
  • Write about gratitude and thing you are thankful for
  • Mix it up and write different things daily
  • Write about your environment and what's around you
  • Using bullet points for jouranling
